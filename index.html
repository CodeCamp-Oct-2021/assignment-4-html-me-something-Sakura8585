My Favorite Stationary

There are many different types of stationary out there, as a stationary lover these are some of my favorites.

Frixion Pens

Frixion pens are one of my favorites because they come in such beatiful colors while also being erasable like a pencil. I really enjoy taking notes with these pens because I can color and style them without having to worry about making mistakes.


Kokuyo Campus Smartring Binder

This binder is a favorite of mine because it has the thinness and portability of a notebook. Another great part of it is that it comes with different types of loose leaf paper for different types of note taking.


Zebra Midliner Highlighter

These highlighters are amazing, the colors are immaculate and it writes so perfectly. You can use the highlighters for other things like coloring. They come with a chisel marker on one side and a fine point on the either, and also have brush marker for calligraphy like writing..
